What is The Great Covid Survey?

The Great Covid Survey is a survey given to people who have tested positive for Covid 19 to see if we can tease out correlations between lifestyle, diet, self-treatments and adverse reactions to the disease.

  • Covid has been affecting individuals with a huge variety of outcomes ranging from asymptomatic to fatality.
  • Outside of a few medical conditions (diabetes, hypertension, lung issues) and demographic information (age, gender) we do not have a lot of information as to who is more susceptible and why.

If we can get better data on what lifestyles, preconditions and demographics correlate to better outcomes we may be able to find better treatments and do a better job of separating out sensitive and non-sensitive people.

Who Can Participate?

The survey is for people who have tested positive for the CoronaVirus through an official test, ideally people who recently received a positive result.

How Does It Work?

Enter your phone number in the form above. You will receive a total of 4 survey requests via text message over the course of two weeks (one main and 3 short followups). All you need to do is complete the surveys!

Why should I participate?

Be a real-world hearo and save lives. The data from your experiences may help us unlock what causes mild and tragic Covid outcomes.

Frequenty Asked Questions

  • Why did you make The Great Covid Survey?

    Our mission is to save lives by helping identify whether specific types of lifestyles, supplements and medications may correlate with better and worse outcomes. We are hoping that by gathering data from thousands of people who have tested positive we can analyize the data and find correlations that help save lives.

  • When can I see the results?

    We would love to have some insights in a matter of weeks. Our goal is to have thousands of people fill out the data and analyze it shortly after we receive outcome data.

  • Are you going to share my data?

    Anonymous data may be shared with researchers and data analysts to help determine correlations. All personal information is separated out. Our system generates a unique random ID for each participant and all analysis will be done using those anonymous ids. We do store your telephone number in a separate contact database and any linkage between the id and the telephone number will be removed at the conclusion of the surveys.
